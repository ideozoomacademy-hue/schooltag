📖 How to Use the Soroban

🟡
Heaven Bead (Top): Worth 5. Click to move DOWN toward the bar to activate it.
🟤
Earth Beads (Bottom 4): Each worth 1. Click to move UP toward the bar. The 1st bead nearest the bar = 1, 2nd = 2, etc.
📐
Column Values: Right column = ones, next = tens, then hundreds and so on (up to trillions!).
💡
Example — Show 7: Move the heaven bead DOWN (=5) + move 2 earth beads UP (=2) → total 7.
🔄
Smart Click: Clicking the 3rd earth bead automatically moves beads 1, 2, and 3 all at once!
