3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide for the following purposes only:

  • To create and deliver your label PDF — Your label details are used to generate a personalised PDF which is sent to you via WhatsApp or email
  • To process and verify your payment — Your UPI transaction ID is used to confirm that payment has been received before releasing your product
  • To provide customer support — If you contact us on WhatsApp or email, we use your contact details to respond to your query
  • To improve our services — Anonymous usage data from Google Analytics helps us understand which features are most useful
  • To send order confirmations — After a successful order, we may send a confirmation message via WhatsApp

We do NOT use your information for: targeted advertising, selling to third parties, sending unsolicited promotional messages, or any purpose beyond delivering your order and providing support.

🍪
4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

SchoolTag uses the following tracking tools to understand website usage and improve our services:

  • Google Analytics (GA4): We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about how visitors use our website — pages visited, time spent, device type, country. This data does not identify you personally. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.
  • Google AdSense: We display Google AdSense advertisements on our website. Google may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history. See Google's advertising privacy policy for details.
  • Local Storage: Our label maker tool uses your browser's local storage to temporarily save your label preferences (name, class, theme). This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. However, some features of the website may not work correctly if cookies are disabled.

👧
5. Children's Privacy

SchoolTag is a service designed for school children, used by their parents and guardians. We take children's privacy extremely seriously.

  • Children do not create accounts or directly provide their personal information to us
  • All transactions and data entry are done by parents or guardians on behalf of their children
  • Children's photos uploaded for label creation are processed entirely within the user's browser and are NOT transmitted to or stored on our servers
  • We do not knowingly collect any personal data directly from children under 13
  • If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete it
👪 For Parents: You have full control over your child's data. You can contact us at any time to request that we delete any information associated with your order.
🤝
6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Google Services: We use Google Analytics for website analytics and Google AdSense for advertising. These services may collect anonymised usage data as described in their respective privacy policies
  • Google Apps Script: Order details (name, school, class, transaction ID) are submitted to a Google Apps Script backend to log orders and trigger PDF delivery via email. This data is stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to DJ Groups
  • WhatsApp: When you contact us or we deliver your product via WhatsApp, that communication is subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal processes
🔐
7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • Our website is served over HTTPS — all data transmitted between your browser and our website is encrypted
  • Order data stored in Google Sheets is protected by Google's security infrastructure and accessible only to authorised DJ Groups personnel
  • We do not store payment card numbers, bank account details, or full UPI IDs
  • Child photos are processed in your browser and are NOT uploaded to or stored on any server

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • Right to Correction: You can ask us to correct inaccurate information

  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using browser settings or the Google opt-out tool
  • Right to Complain: If you have concerns about how we handle your data, you can contact us directly or approach the relevant data protection authority in India
