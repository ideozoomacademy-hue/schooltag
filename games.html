Get all 4 tokens home first to win! 🏠
📖 How to Play Ludo
🎯 Goal: Get all 4 of your tokens to the centre HOME first!
🎲 Roll 6 to bring a token out of your coloured base
🔄 Roll 6 again = roll once more (bonus turn!)
🏃 Move tokens clockwise around the board by your dice number
💀 Safe squares (starred) = cannot be captured
🏠 Home column = coloured path leading to centre — only your colour can enter!
🏆 Win when all 4 tokens reach the centre triangle!
